About Us

Rosales National High School is a prominent educational institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development. Located in Rosales, Pangasinan, it has established itself as a cornerstone of education in the community. The school boasts a dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and a diverse range of academic programs catering to students' varied interests and talents. With a focus on cultivating well-rounded individuals, Rosales National High School actively promotes extracurricular activities, fostering a vibrant environment that nurtures not only intellectual growth but also social and personal development. It stands as a symbol of educational progress, shaping the future of its students and contributing significantly to the educational landscape in the region.

The Accountancy, Business, and Management Strand offers students the fundamentals of business in the 21st-century era. This strand will unmask the different theories, perceptions, and concepts in business, accountancy, marketing, advertising, and entrepreneurship to prepare the learners for real-life situations and further studies.
